Who we are

We are a global community of worker cooperatives, specialized in the development of digital technology, communication and design, located in 17 different countries (and growing), working on projects with an international scope.

This translates into the permanent possibility of scaling the team to work on projects of dimensions that require it. It also allows us to assemble interdisciplinary remote teams with specialized technical profiles according to the needs of each project.

Our philosophy

ITCC is a collaborative project that aims to scale worker-ownership in the name of a more egalitarian society. Whether you are a small cooperative or a larger institution, if you are seeking a skilled technical team that values democracy and practices care, ITCC can help you get in touch with a team of professionals who align with your goals and can provide for the needs of your project.

Key Specters:

  • Create sustainable and healthy work environment that helps projects and teams to thrive.
  • Learn from each other and innovate together by having a hub for knowledge transfer.
  • Use the different approach of each cooperative as a unique point of view that fulfill gaps.
  • Technical partner who transform ideas into digital experience by building multidisciplinary teams and have the ability to scale
